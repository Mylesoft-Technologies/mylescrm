## 🌍 About MylesCRM

MylesCRM is a powerful yet intuitive AI-powered customer relationship management platform designed for East African businesses. From lead generation to deal closure and post-sale relationship management, MylesCRM gives sales teams the tools they need to grow revenue and retain customers.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **Lead Management** — Capture, score, and nurture leads through the sales funnel
- **Pipeline Management** — Visual deal pipelines with drag-and-drop stage management
- **Contact Management** — Rich customer profiles with interaction history
- **AI Sales Insights** — Myles AI predicts deal closure probability and next best actions
- **Task & Activity Tracking** — Calls, emails, meetings, and follow-up reminders
- **Email Integration** — Two-way email sync and automated email sequences
- **Reporting & Analytics** — Sales performance dashboards and revenue forecasting
- **M-Pesa Integration** — Accept payments directly within the CRM
- **Mobile App** — Full-featured mobile CRM for field sales teams
- **Multi-currency** — KES, USD, UGX, TZS, and more
